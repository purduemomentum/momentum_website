Our Story

Momentum at Purdue was founded in Spring 2025 by international students from diverse academic backgrounds, who understand firsthand the challenges of finding career opportunities, building a community, and navigating involvement on campus — especially as minorities and women in STEM. Because of this, we are committed to creating an inclusive, supportive environment where all students, regardless of background, can thrive, gain industry-relevant experience, and feel empowered in their professional journeys.
